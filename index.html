About Us

Aara Beauty Saloon is a premium beauty parlour located in Ghala, Muscat, offering exceptional services in a calm, aesthetic, and welcoming environment.

We provide quality hair, nail, and skin services. What sets Aara apart is our commitment to excellence in one luxurious location.

Our experienced staff, including Kavitha, Ancy, and the team, deliver professional and friendly service, ensuring every client leaves feeling pampered and radiant.

We specialize in facials, pedicures, manicures, hair treatments, eyebrow shaping, saree draping, and more.

Open Daily: 10 AM – 10 PM
