About Us

Aara Beauty Saloon is a premium beauty parlour located in Ghala, Muscat, offering exceptional services in a calm, aesthetic, and welcoming environment.

We will provide quality hair, nail, and skin services. What will set Aara Beauty Saloon apart from the competition is our commitment to providing all of these services in one convenient location.

Our experienced staff, including Kavitha, Ancy, and the team, provide professional and friendly service, ensuring every client leaves feeling pampered and satisfied.

We specialize in facials, pedicures, manicures, hair treatments, eyebrow shaping, saree draping, and more.

Open Daily: 10 AM – 10 PM
